Our Process

The Peer Review Standard

Our specialized 10-reviewer verification workflow ensures the highest levels of scientific rigour and publication credibility — from first submission to global distribution.

Step 01

Submission

Authors upload manuscripts with comprehensive metadata, automatic formatting, and structure validation.

Step 02

Screening

Editorial pre-screening handles structural validations and filters manuscripts before expert allocation.

Step 03

Expert Matching

Advanced algorithm matches 10 peer-review experts based on keywords, citations, and domain background.

Step 04

Evaluation

Assigned reviewers evaluate manuscripts on clarity, structure, methodology, and technical novelty.

Step 05

Publication

Papers scoring 8/10+ average are published and distributed internationally under open-access license.
