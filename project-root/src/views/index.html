The Peer Review Standard

Our specialized 10-reviewer verification workflow ensures the highest levels of scientific rigor and publication credibility.

1. Submission

Authors upload raw manuscripts with comprehensive metadata, automatic formatting, and structure checks.

2. Screening

Pre-screening review handles baseline structural validations and filters papers before expert allocation.

3. AI Matching

Advanced matching algorithm matches 10 matching peer-review experts based on keywords and backgrounds.

4. Evaluation

Assigned reviewers evaluate manuscripts on clarity, structure, methodology, and technical novelty.

5. Publication

Approved papers scoring 8/10+ average are published and distributed internationally under open-access.
