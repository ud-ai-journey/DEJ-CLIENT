Author Guidelines

Welcome, future contributor! Our goal is to make the publication process as smooth and transparent as possible. These guidelines are designed to help you prepare your manuscript for submission to Digital Evolution Journal.

Scope of the Journal

We publish original research articles, review articles, and case studies in all areas of technology and engineering. We are particularly interested in interdisciplinary work that pushes the boundaries of current knowledge. Before submitting, please ensure your work aligns with our focus on advancing technical research excellence.

Manuscript Preparation

To ensure a consistent and high-quality publication, we ask that you format your manuscript according to the following guidelines:

Title

Should be concise, informative, and no longer than 15 words.

Abstract

A structured summary of your research (250-300 words) including Background, Methods, Results, and Conclusion.

Keywords

4-6 keywords that represent the main content of your manuscript.

Main Body

Structured with an Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, and Conclusion. The manuscript should be between 4000 and 8000 words.

References

Formatted in IEEE style. A minimum of 30 references is recommended.

Figures and Tables

High-resolution (300 DPI) and clearly labeled, with captions. Must be submitted as separate files.

Language

All manuscripts must be written in clear and concise English. Non-native English speakers are encouraged to have their manuscripts proofread before submission.

Submission Process

All manuscripts must be submitted through our online portal. The submission process consists of the following steps:

1

Register or Login

Register or log in to your author account.

2

Complete Submission Form

Provide details about the authors, manuscript title, abstract, and keywords.

3

Upload Files

Upload your manuscript files (main body, figures, tables, and supplementary materials).

4

Review and Confirm

Review and confirm your submission.

After submission, you will receive a confirmation email with a manuscript ID. You can track the status of your submission through your author dashboard. Please visit our Submit Paper page for more details.

Peer Review Process

We employ a rigorous double-blind peer review process, with a 10-reviewer system to ensure the highest quality standards. Our AI-powered matching system assigns expert reviewers to evaluate your work on originality, methodology, clarity, and impact.

Possible Outcomes

Accept

The manuscript is accepted for publication without any changes.

Minor Revisions

The manuscript is accepted after minor revisions.

Major Revisions

The manuscript requires major revisions and another round of peer review.

Reject

The manuscript is rejected.

You can learn more about our process on our homepage.

Ethical Guidelines

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of publication ethics. Plagiarism, data fabrication, and other forms of academic misconduct are strictly prohibited. All submissions are screened for plagiarism using industry-standard software.

Read Publication Ethics Policy

Authorship and Contributorship

All listed authors must have made a significant intellectual contribution to the research. The corresponding author is responsible for ensuring that all authors have approved the final version of the manuscript.
